Applications

The Downtown Ithaca Alliance is pleased to announce the 35th Annual Apple Harvest Festival Presented by Tompkins Trust Company. Last year was such a wonderful success, we are looking forward to working with you all again this year. Attracting an audience of over 35,000 people, the festival will be held on:

September 29 (Friday, 12 - 6pm)
September 30 (Saturday, 10am - 6pm)
October 1 (Sunday, 10am - 6pm)

The deadline for entries is Friday, September 1 2017. Acceptance emails will be sent on a rolling basis ending in the first week of September. Applications received after the deadline will be placed on a waiting list in the order in which they are received.

We acknowledge that Yom Kippur 2017 is on this weekend this year. We want to assure you that while we cannot move our festival from the first weekend of October due to a number of production circumstances beyond our control, we have been working hard the past few months to make the Sunday of this year special! If you are a vendor that can only participate on Sunday as a 1 day vendor because you are observing the holiday on Friday and Saturday, please reach out directly to eventsdowntownithaca@gmail.com!

If you should have any questions about any of these guidelines, please do not hesitate to contact me. We are looking forward to working with all of you! Once accepted, a more detailed vendor guidelines document will be sent to you!
