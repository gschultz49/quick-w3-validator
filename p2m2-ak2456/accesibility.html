Accesibility

We have made a map where you can see the different areas and the information point. There are two parking garages located near the festival, however due to the enormous turn-out last year, we strongly recommend you to go by foot or public transportation. The main bus station is located near the Seneca Garage where routes 30, 70, 72, 90, 92 all go to the Cornell Campus.
